How to Play

The goal of this game is to collide with the other player so that you are higher than them.

To move forward, JUMP to go into the air and then KICK to start kicking downwards to your opponents.

If you're on the ground, you can also kick to jump backwards.

Controls:

Gracie:

Jump: A
Kick: S

Nicky:

Jump: K
Kick: L
